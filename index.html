About Us

We are a passionate group of students from the Queen Elizabeth College dedicated to designing, manufacturing, and racing miniature F1 cars as part of the F1 in Schools competition. Our goal is to inspire future engineers and innovators while pushing the boundaries of what's possible.

Through this program, we gain invaluable experience in CAD/CAM, aerodynamics, project management, and teamwork.
